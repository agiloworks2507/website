AgiloWorks is a software company that is helping its customers in their digital transformation journey in Agile way. Because we know how to design and build highly scalable distributed systems, using Agile development methodologies.

BestPik is an online Grocery and Retail e-commerce marketplace in India.

ApzPik is a mobile software platform for small & medium retailers to launch their mobile apps in minutes.

About 300+ customers are gaining by doing business on BestPik & ApzPik platforms. Customers can use this platform and quickly ramp-up their business and increase customers reach.

We are dealing and solving our customer's problem in terms of software development, providing technical consultation, application support and maintenance. We provide primarily the below type of services under this vertical:

Architecture consulting
Development using various Open Source platforms
Open source integration
Expertise in Microservice Architecture

With our Testing COE, we are providing independent offshore software testing to product and services companies, ranging from the Fortune 500s to start-up companies. AgiloWorks has an added advantage of applying strong development expertise to its testing services primarily around automation and API testing. Within short span of inception, we have developed an automation tool under the brand name of TestRobo that can be used by people who don’t have any coding experience. The product is been used by various customers to automate their flows very quickly helping them in quick turnaround in regression testing time.

Established in 2016, AgiloWorks has grown quickly in all these areas and is planning to insert its feet with Fortune 500 companies and start-ups.
