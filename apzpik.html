Why ApzPik?

Creating your own app can cost you anywhere between Rs. 2 lakhs to 1 crore depending on the complexity. There are many complexities of making your own app. It’s not as easy as creating your own website. Even if you create your own app, consumers don’t like to download individual apps. Apzpik has solved this problem by giving businesses a Tiny App within minutes and giving consumers a ApzPik App through which they can access of many favorite and trusted local store Apps. Businesses can manage your own Tiny App without any external help and without knowing any technology.

Your Business Name and Logo

Your tiny app symbolizes your brand exclusively. World will know your business through your name and logo.

Manage Your Catalog Online

ApzPik provides you sophisticated next gen ecommerce platform to publish your product catalog to whole world.

Create a Customer Database

Using ApzPik platform you are the exlusive custodian of your customer database. You can reach them out with your stores offers and promotion in a matter of seconds.

Notify Your Customer Real Time

Reach out to your customers and tell them about your exclusive deals and offers. ApzPik bring your customers more closer to you.

Take order online

Ask your customer to order from you through your tiny app. ApzPik provide you fully integrated Order management features, to manage your customers orders.
